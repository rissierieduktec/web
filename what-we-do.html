WHAT WE DO

Our Programs

Pillar 1: Innovation & Future Skills

Democratizing access to the digital economy.

1

Tech Explorers: Coding & Logic

We introduce children to computational thinking using playful platforms like Scratch and Minecraft Education. We develop the algorithmic logic needed to solve complex problems from an early age.

2

AI for Good Initiative

A pioneering program teaching the fundamentals of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. Students learn how to use these tools ethically to create innovative solutions for local problems.

3

Global English Bridge

More than a language class, this is an immersion into technical English. We teach the essential terminology of the digital and tech world, equipping students with the "lingua franca" needed to work and compete globally.

Pillar 2: Health & Well-being

Ensuring the conditions to thrive.

4

Fuel for Learning

We combat food insecurity through supplementary school nutrition programs. We ensure no student faces the challenge of learning on an empty stomach, improving their concentration and cognitive development.

5

Healthy Habits & Hygiene

Preventive workshops and basic health campaigns (vision, dental, and personal hygiene). We address the invisible physical barriers that hinder learning, ensuring children are healthy and school-ready.

6

Mind & Heart Resilience

Safe spaces for the development of emotional intelligence. We offer tools to manage stress and emotions, strengthening the mental health of children living in vulnerable contexts.

Pillar 3: Leadership & Advocacy

Empowering the leaders of tomorrow.

7

Civic Voice Academy

We train youth in human rights, public speaking, and active citizenship. We empower them to dialogue with local authorities and become effective advocates for their community's needs.

8

Lead Tomorrow Mentorship

We connect outstanding students with professionals and leaders in the tech industry. Through inspiring mentorships, we expand their vision of what is possible to achieve in their professional future.

9

Community Changemakers

Project-based learning where students apply their technology and leadership skills to design and execute a real-world project that benefits their neighborhood or school.
