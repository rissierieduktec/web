Where We Work

From the cloud forests of Francisco Morazán to the valleys of El Paraíso, we are planting seeds of change.

Our Current Pilot Programs

We are currently operating active pilot groups in two key regions of Honduras.

Danlí, El Paraíso

  • Group Size: 25 Children
  • Format: Virtual Classes & Digital Learning
  • Focus: Bridging the digital divide in rural areas.

In Dalin, we are proving that distance is no barrier to quality education. Through our virtual classroom initiative, 25 children are connecting with world-class mentors and learning resources previously unimaginable in their community.

Santa Lucía, F.M.

  • Program: Comprehensive Community Pilot
  • Key Initiatives: Nutrition, English, and Coding
  • Impact: 100% Retention Rate

Our flagship pilot in Santa Lucía serves as the model for our holistic approach. Here, we integrate nutritional support with high-impact educational programs, creating a thriving ecosystem where children are healthy, engaged, and learning fast.
